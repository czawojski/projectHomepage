About me

My good friend Ed Abbey once said: “Haven't seen a real fire or smoke yet. One craves a little excitement around this joint.” Eager for some excitement myself, I've been making campfires ever since he said that. It's not the most profitable business--hell, I actually do it for free--but it has its rewards. Warm hands, hot food, and purifying hardwood smoke, to name a few.
